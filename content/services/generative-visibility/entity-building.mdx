In the legacy era of search, we optimized for words. In the AI economy, we optimize for **Entities**. 

Search engines like Google and Answer Engines like Perplexity no longer see your site as a collection of pages, but as a relationship between defined nodes in a knowledge graph. **Entity Building** is the process of establishing and solidifying your brand's authoritative footprint within this semantic ecosystem.
